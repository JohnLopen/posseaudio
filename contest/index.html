DigiTour Posse Audio Video Contest:

Posse Audio is awarding 10 Posse Personal Monitor Systems and 1 Grand Prize YouTube All-Star VIP Weekend Recording Experience for the Best Posse Video Submission!

Posse Audio Official Contest Rules:

Posse Audio (“Sponsor”) is offering one (1) grand prize winner a chance to win a weekend trip (roundtrip airfare and accommodations (1) one person) to Los Angeles to record a song at Flying Pig Studios to be released as a bonus track on the DigiTour 2012 Official Compilation Album. Ten (10) finalists will receive a Posse Audio Personal In-Ear Monitor System and have their videos featured on The DigiTour Youtube channel.

NO PURCHASE NECESSARY TO ENTER OR WIN. MAKING A PURCHASE DOES NOT INCREASE YOUR CHANCES OF WINNING.  The “Posse Audio” Contest is open to legal residents of the United States, including the District of Columbia, who are 18 years of age or older.

HOW TO ENTER: Beginning March 7th, 2012 through 12:00 AM Pacific Time on April 15, 2012 (the “entry period”) submit a video on Youtube as a response to the official Posse Audio Contest Announcement video //www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZI8NEWJGFl8 1-2 minutes in length featuring the product Posse Audio. The entries will be judged on appropriateness and at the sole discretion of DigiTour Media and Posse Audio The decision of the judges will be final and binding in all matters.  The one (1) entry chosen per category by the DigiTour Media and Posse Audio judges will be posted on the www.PosseAudio.com website and at youtube.com/Posse Audio and youtube.com/thedigitour on April 25, 2012.

The top (10) ten finalists will also receive a Posse Audio Personal Monitor System.

No transfer, substitution or cash will be awarded in lieu of the stated prize.  The prize is non-transferable, non-substitutable and non-endorsable.
